About Me

I am Ibrahim Al Sharkawi, a Senior Graphic Designer and Full Stack Web Developer with over 5 years of experience in branding, advertising, and promotional design, complemented by 3 years of experience in full-stack web development.

I constantly work on developing my skills and following the latest trends in both design and web development. I believe that good design is not only about aesthetic appearance, but must also be functional, user-friendly, and technically sound.

During my professional career, I have worked with a variety of clients from different sectors, helping them achieve their goals through outstanding designs and robust web solutions that meet their needs and exceed their expectations.
